masaladosa:

Ingredients:

  • For the Dosa Batter:
    • 1 cup rice, 1/4 cup urad dal (black gram), 1/4 tsp fenugreek seeds (optional), Salt to taste.
  • For the Potato Filling:
    • 4 large potatoes, boiled and mashed, 1 onion, finely chopped, 2-3 green chilies, finely chopped, 1 tsp mustard seeds, 1 tsp cumin seeds, A pinch of asafoetida (hing), 1/2 tsp turmeric powder, A few curry leaves, Salt to taste, 2 tbsp oil.
  • For the Dosa Masala:
    • 1 tsp mustard seeds 1 tsp urad dal 1 tsp chana dal 1/2 tsp asafoetida (hing) 1/2 tsp turmeric powder 1/2 cup chopped onions 1/2 cup chopped tomatoes 1/2 cup chopped bell peppers (optional) Salt to taste.
  • For the Coconut Chutney:
    • 1 cup grated coconut, 2-3 green chilies, 1/2 inch piece of ginger, A handful of fresh coriander leaves, Salt to taste, Water as needed.
  • For the Sambar:
    • 1/2 cup toor dal (pigeon pea lentils), 1 small onion, chopped, 1 small tomato, chopped, 1 small carrot, chopped, 1 small potato, chopped, A handful of green beans, chopped, 1/4 cup tamarind pulp, 1 tbsp sambar powder, 1/2 tsp turmeric powder, Salt to taste, A few curry leaves, 1 tbsp oil.

Instructions:

  1. For the Dosa Batter:
    Rinse the rice, urad dal, and fenugreek seeds (if using) separately and soak them in water for 6-8 hours. Grind them together to form a smooth batter. Add water as needed to get a slightly thick, but pourable consistency. Add salt and mix well. Leave the batter to ferment in a warm place for 8-12 hours or overnight.
  2. For the Potato Filling:
    Heat oil in a pan and add mustard seeds. Once they splutter, add cumin seeds, assafoetida, and curry leaves. Add chopped onions and green chilies. Sauté until the onions turn golden brown. Add turmeric powder and mashed potatoes. Mix well. Season with salt and cook for a few more minutes. Set aside.
  3. For the Coconut Chutney:
    Blend together grated coconut, green chilies, ginger, coriander leaves, and salt with a little water to make a smooth chutney. Adjust consistency as desired.
  4. For the Sambar:
    Pressure cook toor dal until soft. Mash it and set aside. In a large pot, heat oil. Add mustard seeds, curry leaves, chopped onions, and sauté until onions turn translucent. Add chopped tomatoes, sambar powder, turmeric powder, and salt. Cook until the tomatoes turn soft. Add tamarind pulp, cooked dal, and chopped vegetables. Add enough water to get the desired consistency. Cook until the vegetables are tender.
  5. For the Dosa Masala:
    Heat oil in a pan. Add mustard seeds, urad dal, chana dal, and assafoetida. Let them splutter and turn golden brown. Add chopped onions and sauté until translucent. Add tomatoes and bell peppers (if using) and cook until soft. Add turmeric powder and salt. Mix well. Add the prepared potato filling and mix everything together. Cook for a few more minutes and set aside.
  6. for making masaladosa
    Heat a non-stick dosa tava or griddle. Grease it lightly with oil. Pour a ladleful of dosa batter onto the center of the tava and spread it in a circular motion to make a thin dosa. Drizzle a little oil around the edges and cook until the dosa turns golden brown. Place a portion of the masala filling in the center of the dosa and fold it over. Serve hot with coconut chutney and sambar. Enjoy your masala dosa!.
