ButterChicken:

Ingredients:

  • For the Marinade:
    • 500g boneless chicken pieces, 1/2 cup yogurt, 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste, 1 tablespoon red chili powder (adjust to taste), 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder, Salt to taste.
  • For the Gravy:
    • 2 tablespoons butter, 1 tablespoon oil, 2 large onions, finely chopped, 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste, 2 large tomatoes, pureed, 1 tablespoon tomato paste (optional, for extra richness), 1 tablespoon red chili powder (adjust to taste), 1 teaspoon garam masala, 1 teaspoon coriander powder, 1/2 teaspoon cumin powder, 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder, 1/2 cup heavy cream, Salt to taste, Water as needed.
  • For Garnish:
    • Fresh coriander leaves, chopped.
